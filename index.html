Project ASHER – Adaptive Student Helper & Educational AI

ASHER is an interactive K-12 assistant featuring a friendly avatar, voice+text conversation, and a robust knowledge base aligned to state & standardized curricula (MEAP, ACT, SAT). Parents customize cultural, religious, or ethical filters; ASHER teaches problem-solving, not answer-copying, and prepares students through gamified practice and formative feedback.

Seeking pre-seed partners to complete MVP, safety audits, and pilot deployments with Michigan school districts.

Project Genesis – Privacy-Preserving Synthetic Data Platform

Genesis is a lake-native engine that ingests sensitive enterprise data, auto-curates schemas, and produces high-fidelity synthetic twins while enforcing differential-privacy guarantees and eliminating hallucinations. It streams synthetic datasets back to S3 for AI training and testing at >1 TB / hour and <0.5 % schema violations.

Seeking seed funding for scaling tests, compliance certification, and early-adopter integrations in health-tech and fintech.
