About Aldin AI

Aldin AI LLC, founded in 2025 in White Lake, Michigan, is an early-stage technology company focused on developing ethical, AI-driven platforms that serve both enterprise and educational markets. The company is currently led by founder Salah Mokhayesh, an experienced data architect and technologist with a track record at major organizations including General Motors, Rocket Mortgage, and OnStar.

Aldin AI’s flagship enterprise product is a privacy-preserving synthetic data engine designed to support AI/ML development in regulated industries. The system ingests structured data from sources like Amazon S3, auto-curates schemas, and generates synthetic records that maintain fidelity while eliminating sensitive information. This enables enterprises to accelerate model development, reduce privacy risks, and meet compliance standards such as HIPAA and GDPR.

In parallel, Aldin AI is developing Project ASHER, an AI-powered educational assistant for K–12 students. ASHER features a speech-enabled avatar, aligned with standardized curricula and customizable by parents to reflect cultural, religious, and personal values. The platform is designed to support problem-solving skills, safeguard against inappropriate content, and prepare students for exams like MEAP, ACT, and SAT—making high-quality, tailored learning accessible at home or in school.

Mission: Aldin AI’s mission is to build trusted, human-centered AI solutions that protect privacy, empower learning, and close critical access gaps. Through innovation in both enterprise data and personalized education, the company seeks to redefine what responsible AI looks like across industries.
