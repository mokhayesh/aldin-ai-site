Introducing Aldin Mini Go

Your Private AI Workstation on a USB

Aldin Mini Go is a portable AI assistant designed for local AI chat, offline-capable workflows, web search when connected, document analysis, local memory, knowledge-base tools, and professional deliverables.

Offline when you need privacy. Online when you need fresh information. Working USB prototypes are already running. Kickstarter campaign coming soon.

Mission

Aldin AI pioneers ethical, high-impact artificial-intelligence solutions that unlock the full value of data while empowering learners and protecting privacy. We bridge frontier research and practical deployment to create tools that are safe, inclusive, and future-proof. Aldin AI is not only a think tank, but an execution hub delivering the most relevant ways to utilize AI.

Aldin Mini Go brings that mission into a portable product: a private AI workstation on a USB for local AI chat, offline-capable workflows, web search, document intelligence, local memory, and professional deliverables.
