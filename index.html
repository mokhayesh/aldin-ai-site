About Aldin AI

Aldin AI is a Michigan-based startup building cutting-edge, privacy-first artificial intelligence platforms for real-world challenges. From data governance to education technology, we deliver scalable, secure, and human-aligned solutions for today's evolving needs.

Project ASHER

Project ASHER is an interactive AI-powered assistant for K–12 students. Designed with robust safeguards and adaptable cultural alignment, it helps families tailor educational experiences to their values. With speech-to-text, personalized test prep (e.g., SAT, ACT, MEAP), and a possible holographic interface, ASHER supports children in learning how to think—not just what to think.
