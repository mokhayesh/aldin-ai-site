Ethical AI for Data & Learners

Mission

Aldin AI pioneers ethical, privacy-first artificial intelligence that empowers both enterprises and learners. We envision a future where synthetic data unlocks innovation without compromising personal privacy, and where every student can access culturally attuned, AI-driven tutoring that nurtures curiosity and success.

Project ASHER  |  Seeking Seed Funding

A speech-enabled K-12 learning companion with a friendly avatar that:

  • Aligns to MEAP, ACT, SAT, and local curricula.
  • Lets parents set cultural, religious, or ethical boundaries for content.
  • Guides students to solve problems—never just giving answers.
  • Runs safe-content filters and prepares learners via interactive voice & text.
Future roadmap includes mobile AR and holographic interfaces for an immersive classroom-anywhere experience.

Project Genesis  |  Seeking Seed Funding

Genesis is our real-time synthetic-data engine that:

  • Ingests raw data from S3 (or any object storage) and auto-detects complex schemas.
  • Uses a hybrid GenAI & deterministic pipeline to generate privacy-preserving synthetic datasets (≈1 TB/hr <0.5% violations).
  • Redistributes data to isolated sandboxes, enabling safe AI/ML training, A/B testing, and analytics even outside data-lake environments.
Genesis eliminates privacy bottlenecks, accelerates model iteration, and keeps regulators happy.
