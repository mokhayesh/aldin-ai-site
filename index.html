About Aldin AI

Aldin AI LLC is a Michigan-based AI company founded in 2025. We specialize in developing generative AI systems that prioritize safety, privacy, and alignment. Our mission is to empower innovation through AI that adapts to context, respects privacy, and scales across industries.

Project ASHER – Personalized AI for K–12 Education

Project ASHER is an AI-powered educational assistant for K–12 students. Featuring a speech-enabled avatar, ASHER offers curriculum-aligned tutoring tailored by parental, cultural, and ethical preferences. Designed with robust safeguards, it prepares students for standardized exams like the MEAP, ACT, and SAT while maintaining interactive, age-appropriate engagement.

Project NEXUS – Hybrid Synthetic Data Platform

Project NEXUS delivers privacy-preserving synthetic data that mirrors enterprise data lake structures. It combines deterministic code with GenAI to preserve relational accuracy while preventing re-identification. Our hallucination-mitigation loop ensures that generated data remains valid and regulation-compliant.
