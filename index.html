About Aldin AI

Aldin AI is a Michigan-based startup building privacy-first generative AI solutions. Our focus is creating synthetic data pipelines and ethical AI tools tailored for education and regulated industries.

Project ASHER

Project ASHER is a K–12 virtual assistant designed with speech and visual interaction. ASHER helps students prepare for standardized tests (e.g., MEAP, SAT), adapts to family values and culture, and ensures safe, engaging learning. It supports speech-to-text and text-to-speech interactions and can eventually integrate with mobile or holographic interfaces.
