Project ASHER – Adaptive Student Helper & Educational AI

An interactive K‑12 assistant with avatar, voice + text, and curriculum‑aligned knowledge base. Parents can tailor cultural and ethical filters; ASHER teaches problem‑solving and prepares students for MEAP, ACT, and SAT.

Interface Capabilities

  • Journal — View saved reflections and learning history
  • Preferences — Customize ASHER’s behavior, filters, and difficulty
  • Home — Return to dashboard
  • ASHER Avatar — Friendly visual guide and learning companion
  • Greeting Message — ASHER’s welcome prompt
  • Upload Button — Submit worksheets or images for analysis
  • Grade Selector — Aligns responses with selected academic level
  • Subject Selector — Switches ASHER’s knowledge domain
  • Language Selector — Controls response language
  • Voice Selector — Chooses spoken voice output
  • Start Quiz Button — Generates instant quiz based on settings
  • Chat Input Field — Type questions for ASHER
  • Send Button — Submit typed question
  • Microphone Toggle — Enable voice input
  • Record Button — Record audio for ASHER to interpret

Journal

ASHER’s journal acts as the student’s growth dashboard. It tracks time‑on‑learning, grade‑level alignment, emerging interests, subject mastery, and accomplishments. This transforms ASHER from a simple tutor into a measurable learning companion.

Preferences

The preferences panel is the governance layer of ASHER. Parents define cultural context, religious sensitivity, political neutrality, orientation filtering, cultural awareness level, and learning style. These settings shape how ASHER teaches, ensuring safety, alignment, and respect for each family’s values.

Seeking pre‑seed partners for MVP completion and pilot deployments.

Project Genesis – Autonomous Data Governance & Synthetic Intelligence Platform

Genesis operates as an AI‑infused impact multiplier, engineered with a governance‑first mindset to accelerate opportunity discovery without exposing sensitive information.

Its synthetic twins preserve statistical truth, enforce differential privacy, and maintain structural integrity with <0.5% schema deviation. Capable of streaming to S3 at over 1 TB/hr, Genesis enables safe experimentation, rapid prototyping, and compliant analytics at scale.
