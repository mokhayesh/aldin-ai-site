Mission

Aldin AI pioneers ethical, privacy-first artificial intelligence that empowers both enterprises and learners. We envision a future where synthetic data unlocks innovation without compromising personal privacy, and where every student can access culturally attuned, AI-driven tutoring that nurtures curiosity and success.

Aldin AI is not only a think-tank but an execution hub on the most relevant ways to utilize AI.

Project ASHER  |  Seeking Seed Funding

  • Speech-enabled K-12 assistant with friendly avatar.
  • Customizable to MEAP, ACT, SAT & family cultural preferences.
  • Problem-solving guidance—never just the answer.
  • Robust content filters & interactive multimodal learning.
  • Roadmap: mobile AR & holographic classroom-anywhere interface.

Project Genesis  |  Seeking Seed Funding

  • Real-time synthetic-data engine: ≈1 TB/hr, <0.5 % schema violations.
  • Ingests raw S3 (or any object store), auto-detects schemas, outputs safe data.
  • Hybrid GenAI + code pipeline with differential-privacy guards.
  • Eliminates privacy bottlenecks for AI/ML, A/B testing & analytics—even beyond data lakes.
