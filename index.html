Our Mission

To build safe, human-centered AI systems that respect privacy, cultural diversity, and real-world integrity while unlocking the full potential of data and personalized education for everyone.

Project ASHER

A futuristic AI-powered learning assistant for K-12 students, ASHER combines speech-to-text, an interactive avatar, and customizable educational content. Parents can tailor instruction to align with values and culture while students prepare for standardized tests like MEAP, ACT, and SAT with real understanding—not just answers. ASHER ensures safety and appropriateness with strong content filters and curriculum alignment.

Project Genesis

Genesis is Aldin AI's synthetic data generation platform. It pulls data from storage (like Amazon S3), understands its schema, generates privacy-preserving synthetic data, and sends it back—automated, continuous, and secure. Designed for data-starved industries (health, finance, defense), Genesis maintains statistical fidelity and supports AI/ML pipelines with zero risk of exposure or hallucination.
